Location Details

1: Chiller Plant and Seawater Pump House

HKUST's campus location provides an excellent opportunity to use a seawater cooling system: an inexpensive and energy-efficient cooling system for centralized air conditioning plants. Compared to an air cooling system, seawater cooling systems save substantial amounts of energy. Water discharge is regularly monitored to assure compliance with environmental regulatory requirements. The large concrete block found about 200 metres offshore marks the seawater intake.
